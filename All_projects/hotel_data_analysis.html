Welcome to this data analysis blog, where we delve into the world of hotels to gain valuable insights that can drive business growth.

By examining a dataset comprising both City and Resort hotels, we aim to uncover trends and patterns in customer behavior. Leveraging Python and popular data analysis libraries, including NumPy, Pandas, Matplotlib, and Seaborn, we will take you through the various stages of the analysis pipeline. From data cleaning and preprocessing to analysis and visualization, we will extract meaningful information to provide actionable recommendations for enhancing hotel performance.

** Some Insights from this analysis**

  • The total number of city hotels are almost double that of Resort hotels.
  • Cancelation rates are much higher in city hotels.
  • From November to March the Resort hotels sees maximum growth in the business.
  • Higher cancelation rates in city hotels but, they are also getting reservations almost double to that of Resort hotels.- the main reason is that City hotels are comparatively cheaper than Resort hotels attracting mass customers and they are more in numbers as well.
